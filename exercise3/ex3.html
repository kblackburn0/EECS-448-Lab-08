Summary


Stephen Robert Irwin (22 February 1962 - 4 September 2006), nicknamed "The Crocodile Hunter", was an Australian zookeeper, television personality, wildlife expert, environmentalist, and conservationist. Irwin achieved worldwide fame from the television series The Crocodile Hunter (1996-2007), an internationally broadcast wildlife documentary series that he co-hosted with his wife Terri. (Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steve_Irwin)
